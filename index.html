FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do you work with smaller brands?

+

Yes. We work with ecommerce brands from $100k/year and up. Our Starter plan is specifically designed to help brands in early growth stages test and prove out the creator channel before scaling. Book a call and we'll tell you honestly if we're the right fit.

How quickly can campaigns start?

+

Onboarding takes 1–2 weeks, after which we move fast. Most brands see their first batch of creators activated and posting within 30 days of kickoff. Timelines vary based on product availability and campaign scope.

Do we need to manage the creators ourselves?

+

No. That's the whole point. We handle creator outreach, briefing, communication, content approval, and follow-up. Your team reviews a monthly report — that's it. We act as a fully embedded creator marketing department.

Do we need to send product for every creator?

+

Typically yes — product seeding is the foundation of authentic creator content. We work with your team to manage inventory allocation smartly, targeting creators with the highest probability of posting and performance. We can also run paid UGC programs if product availability is limited.

How do you track revenue and ROI?

+

Every creator gets a unique affiliate link and promo code. We track clicks, conversions, and revenue through your Shopify or attribution platform. You receive a real-time dashboard and detailed monthly reports showing creator-level performance.

What platforms do creators post on?

+

We work across TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and long-form YouTube depending on your audience and category. We recommend the best channel mix based on your product and where your target customers actually spend time.
